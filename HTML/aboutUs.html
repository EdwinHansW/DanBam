About Us

"Every menu and food ingredients are prepared, selected, and made with the extra care of exceptional Korean Chefs and our Master Head Chef, who has a long history of professional experiences in Korean cuisine for more than 15 years."

DanBam Korean Restaurant was founded by Jeta Nanda, Edwin Hans, and Michael Budiman on October, 6th 2021 signaled with the first restaurant being established at Supermall Karawaci in Tangerang. DanBam Korean Restaurant offers the ideal dining place with solely premium BBQ and A La Carte experience blended with the beautiful, elegant ambiance and the modern atmosphere of the soothing neon illumination.

After a year of serving 15+ authentic Korean menus to 10.000+ customers, DanBam has established its branches at Gading Serpong, Tangerang, and Grand Indonesia Mall in June 2019 and August 2020. DanBam offers the highest quality of service – warm, friendly, and professional. Our staffs are happy to provide the best service to you. The food is fresh consistently as our dedicated staff is always taking excellent care of the quality in our menus for the satisfaction of your dining experience. At DanBam, we are happy to serve you our extraordinarily planned menu including natural and vegetarian dishes to satisfy the taste buds of people who seek unforgettable Korean experiences.
