About Us


DanBam Korean Restaurant offers the ideal dining place with solely premium BBQ and A La Carte experience. The food is fresh and of top quality consistently with the extra care of exceptional Korean Chefs. At DanBam, we are happy to serve you our extraordinarily planned menu including natural and vegetarian dishes to satisfy the taste buds of people who seek Korean experiences.






Recommendation Menu

O-Jing-Uh


Fresh squids are cut into bite-sized pieces then pan stir-fried with spicy gochujang along with gochugaru.

Kang-Poong Sae-Woo


Battered, crispy fried shrimp in a spicy garlic sauce containing hot chili peppers and sprinkles of scallions.

Gop-Chang


Gopchang is a dish made of small intestines of cattle marinated in the seasonings and spices, then grilled over a barbecue
